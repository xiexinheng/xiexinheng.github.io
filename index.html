Seminar Series: Women in Data Science and Mathematics

Machine learning, a powerful and data-driven technology, has been successfully used in diverse fields such as computer vision, digital health, and even science. However, the mathematical foundation and advancements in machine learning may not be as widely recognised. 

The seminar series aims to highlight recent advances in interdisciplinary research on mathematics and data science in machine learning, increase visibility for female and non-binary researchers, and facilitate research discussions among diverse participants. This will serve as a platform for exchanging ideas and networking for future career growth.

Event Format

Our seminar series will be held on the last Wednesday of every month via Zoom meetings. Each session will last approximately one hour and will include a research talk followed by a Q&A session. The participants are welcome to join the informal chat with the organisers and speakers after the talk to foster interaction. To accommodate attendees from different regions, the seminar time may rotate.

To keep regular updates of our seminar series, sign up the mailing list of our seminar series.
